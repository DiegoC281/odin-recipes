Lasagna

World's best Lasagna

The ultimate Lasagna to rule them all, created by italian tire manufacturer Giacomo Pergolini in 1873 in an effort to kill his arch enemy Mario di Trevola by spiking it with cyanide

After his untimely demise en 1897, Giacomo's diary was found by his nephew Luigi Pergolini, who made this recipe into the star of the family restaurant, minus the mortal posion

  • Cheese in industrial quantities
  • Ovo
